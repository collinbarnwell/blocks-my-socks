Hello there!

Directions:

  1. The Space Bar will recalculate the picture.
  2. The arrow keys move the camera parallel to the ground plane.
  3. 'W' and 'S' move the camera up and down.
  4. 'A' and 'D' rotate the camera direction about the vertical axis.
  5. 'Q' and 'E' rotate the camera about the horizontal axis.
  6. 'Z' toggles antialiasing.
  7. Play with the fields below the canvas to adjust the light sources or turn them on/off. Make sure you click somewhere else after clicking one of the checkboxes or editing the fields (to deselecct them), then press "SPACE".
  8. You can also switch scenes with the radio buttons below the canvas. Make sure you click somewhere else after clicking the radio button (to deselect it), then hit "SPACE".
  9. EVERY TIME YOU MAKE A CHANGE, YOU MUST PRESS "SPACE" FOR THE PICTURE TO GET RETRACED.
  10. Hit 'F1' to hide or display these instructions (or 'H', or '1').
  11. Enjoy!
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
